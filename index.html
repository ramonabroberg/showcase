About us

Showcase has been around since 2013 with a strong vision:

  • We want to make it easier for you to find concerts and events anytime, anywhere.
  • We want you to find everything you need in the same place so you can spend your time on more enjoyable things than chasing tickets.
  • We want to offer the best prices possible so nothing can stop you from seeing your favorite band.
  • We want it to feel easy and fun to find the next gig you want to go to!
